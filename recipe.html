Description

Hello! My name is Gavin Chavez and I am passionate about music. I come from an artistic family with my Grandfather being a well known musicician performing on T.V. and my uncles and mother being artists. Growing up I was surrounded by art in one form or another. My brother began playing guitar when I was 7 and since I looked up to him that became my next mission. When I was 10 I started self teaching myself guitar on any guitar I could get my hands on, wether it be my brothers, Grandfathers, or just friends who had one but necer used it. When I was 14 I got my first guitar. I began learning techniques that would help me develop my own personal sound when it comes to music.

At 20 years old I would like to say that I am proud of what I have accomplished and am excited for the future to futher improve myself when it comes to music, music knowledge, and growing as a person.
